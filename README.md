# Biotech & Healthcare Marketing Case Study Repository

## Welcome to My Portfolio!
Hi, I'm a Biotechnology undergraduate student. While I love the science behind life-saving innovations, I am fascinated by the business side of things: **How do we take a complex scientific discovery out of the lab and successfully bring it into the real world?**

I am building this project to learn how marketing, branding, business strategy, and consumer psychology work together in the science, health, and wellness industries. It serves as my self-directed learning journey as I prepare to apply for a Master's degree in Management and Business.

---

## What is Inside This Project?

This repository is organized just like a digital library. Here is where everything lives:
* `/case-studies`: Detailed stories and business breakdowns of global companies.
* `/strategy-frameworks`: Simple visual guides and models showing how business concepts apply to science.
* `/assets`: Graphic charts, positioning maps, and simple dashboards designed on Canva.
* `/templates`: The blank layout I use to keep every new case study looking neat and organized.

## Simple Business Concepts I Am Practicing

In each study, I try to look at three main ideas:
1. **The Translation Problem:** How does a company explain a complicated scientific metric (like protein solubility or clinical trial data) in plain, simple language that a regular consumer can understand and trust?
2. **The Trust Factor:** Science can sometimes cause public fear or skepticism (like GMOs or data privacy). How do brands build emotional safety and trust?
3. **The Competitive Advantage:** Why do customers choose one specific medicine, health app, or food alternative over another?
